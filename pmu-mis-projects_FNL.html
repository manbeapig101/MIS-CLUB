About this website

The MIS Club Projects website showcases prototypes built by students in the Management Information Systems program at Prince Mohammad Bin Fahd University. It is a simple, lightweight site designed for classroom demos and portfolios.

Our goal

  • Provide a single place to browse project prototypes by academic semester.
  • Highlight historical projects that inspired new teams.
  • Keep everything lightweight so it loads quickly on any device.
